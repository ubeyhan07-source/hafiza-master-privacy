Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hafıza Master ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application Hafıza Master (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Hafıza Master is an offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not send any data to external servers.

The only data stored by the App is:

  • Your in-game username (stored locally on your device)
  • Your game scores and leaderboard data (stored locally on your device)
  • Your purchase history for in-app purchases (stored locally on your device)

This data never leaves your device.

2. In-App Purchases

Hafıza Master offers optional in-app purchases (level unlocks) processed exclusively through Google Play In-App Payments. We do not collect or store your payment information. All payment transactions are handled securely by Google Play. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children's Privacy

Hafıza Master is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App operates fully offline and collects no personal data, it is safe for children to use.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

5. Data Security

Since Hafıza Master does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or lost through our App.

6. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

For users in the European Union: Hafıza Master does not process any personal data as defined by the GDPR. No data is collected, stored on servers, or shared with third parties. You have full control over locally stored game data, which can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
